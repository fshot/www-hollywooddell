## Important Note on Retroactivity

As of March 2026, **none of the pending bills are expected to be retroactive**. Homeowners who complete hardening work before legislation passes likely won't be able to claim reimbursement under these programs. However, existing programs (RCDSMM, MySafe:LA) provide free assessments and some cost assistance today.
