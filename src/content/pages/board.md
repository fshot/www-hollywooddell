## President — Alexa Iles (Skarpelos)

Pacific Northwest native who relocated to Hollywood Dell in 2000. Active in community volunteering, serving as HDCA president, Civilian Co-Chair on the Hollywood Community-Police Advisory Board, and member of the Hollywood Bowl Community Advisory Group.

## Vice President — David Brewer

Originally from Lexington, Kentucky. Moved to California after visiting during the 1971 San Fernando earthquake. Has lived in the Dell since 1980 with his husband Jim and their cat Samantha.

## Treasurer — Kelly Ziegler

Longtime Los Angeles resident who moved to the Dell in 1999. Graduate of University of Puget Sound and holds an MBA from Pepperdine University. Works in Finance for Nestlé in Glendale. Active in children's school and nonprofit organizations.

## Secretary — Jan Hohenstein

Grew up in southern Appalachian Mountains. Has worked as a real estate agent for twenty years. Mount Saint Mary's University graduate. Values the tight-knit community atmosphere of the neighborhood.
