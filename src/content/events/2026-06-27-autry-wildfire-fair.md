A major wildfire safety event produced by David Barrett in collaboration with Council District 4, LAFD, and LAPD.

**What to expect:**
- Pancake breakfast
- Live music
- Classroom events and workshops
- LAFD demonstrations
- 20+ vendor booths
- Hollywood Hills Fire Safe Council speaking slot and booth

**Cost:** Free admission

The HDCA Fire Safety Committee will be present. This is a great opportunity to learn about wildfire preparedness and connect with local fire safety organizations.
